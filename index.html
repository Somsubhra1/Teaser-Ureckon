About Us

University of Engineering & Management, Kolkata in association with Indian Space Research Organisation(ISRO), Kolkata and Institute of Engineering & Management, Kolkata is organising its 1st annual Techno-Management festival-“URECKON 2019”. Students and tech enthusiasts from various corners of the country are wholeheartedly invited to showcase their innovations, ingenuity and teamwork here. Also, URECKON will be exhibiting application based robots along with ISRO, Kolkata. URECKON presents several Scientific Models, Robotic events, Coding events, B-events, Guest lectures, Tech Talks, Workshops and many more. URECKON is an opportunity for the young minds to showcase their own creations, techniques and plans in most of the fields of technology and management. Along with the academic events, we also present exciting gaming events too. We are proud to announce the different events on various domains to glorify our festivals.
